Red Team Coordination

How blue teams can work with red teams to improve detection and response.

Agree on scope and rules

Clarify which systems are in scope, testing windows, and acceptable techniques before red team exercises begin.

Share response playbooks

Ensure the blue team has documented steps for identifying, containing, and reporting simulated attacks.

Review findings together

After simulation, review what was detected, what was missed, and how visibility can be improved.
