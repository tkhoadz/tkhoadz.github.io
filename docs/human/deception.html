Deception & Honeypot Awareness

Learn how defenders use deception to detect and slow down attackers.

What is deception?

Deception uses fake systems, credentials, or data to attract attackers and reveal their behavior.

Common defensive tools

  • Honeypots and honeynets
  • Fake credentials or documents
  • Deceptive network services

How defenders use it

Deception helps security teams spot unauthorized access early and study attacker techniques without exposing production systems.
