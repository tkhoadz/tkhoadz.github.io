Phishing Awareness

Learn how to spot phishing emails and protect yourself from credential theft.

Spot malicious emails

  • Check the sender address carefully
  • Look for spelling mistakes and odd formatting
  • Hover over links to verify their destination
  • Be wary of urgent or alarming messages

Safe response

echo "Verify suspicious email details with IT or security before taking action."

Do not click links or download attachments from unknown or unexpected sources.

Defensive actions

  • Enable email filtering and anti-phishing tools
  • Use multi-factor authentication
  • Educate staff with regular phishing simulations
