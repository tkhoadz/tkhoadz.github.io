Social Engineering Awareness

Understand how attackers manipulate people and how defenders can recognize these tactics.

Understand the threat

Social engineering is about exploiting trust, not technology. Awareness is the best defense.

Common indicators

  • Unexpected requests for credentials or sensitive data
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Requests to bypass normal processes
  • Links or attachments from unknown senders

Defensive checklist

  • Verify the sender by a second channel
  • Do not share passwords or OTP codes
  • Use phishing-resistant MFA and security training
  • Report suspicious contacts to security teams
