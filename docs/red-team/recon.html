Reconnaissance and Information Gathering

Authorized red team reconnaissance in a lab or approved environment.

Map the target network

sudo nmap -sn 192.168.1.0/24

Discover live hosts and compare them to the known infrastructure inventory.

Identify open services

sudo nmap -sV -p 1-1000 192.168.1.10

Identify exposed services on an authorized target to inform defensive controls and patches.

Document findings

Record all scanned hosts, services, and any unusual behavior in a report for later analysis.
