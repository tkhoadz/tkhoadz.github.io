Network Audit Basics

Use port and host discovery to validate your network and detect unexpected devices.

Discover online devices

sudo nmap -sn 192.168.1.0/24

Find hosts on your local network and compare them against your inventory.

Audit exposed services

sudo nmap -sV -p 1-1000 192.168.1.10

Inspect open ports and services on a machine you own to detect unexpected exposures.
