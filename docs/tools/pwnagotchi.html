Pwnagotchi Awareness

Understand passive Wi-Fi credential capture tools and how to protect your network against them.

What to watch for

Devices like Pwnagotchi listen to Wi-Fi traffic and capture handshakes. Know the risk it poses to weak or open networks.

Monitor for unknown clients

sudo tcpdump -i wlan0 -n 'arp or icmp' | head

Capture low-volume traffic to see unexpected devices on the wireless network.

Protect your network

Use strong WPA3 or WPA2 passphrases and disable open or poorly secured Wi-Fi networks.
