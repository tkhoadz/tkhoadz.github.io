Malicious USB Awareness

Learn how to recognize and defend against dangerous USB devices and BadUSB-style attacks.

Recognize suspicious USB devices

Be cautious of unknown USB sticks or devices that show up unexpectedly. Do not plug them in unless you trust the source.

Inspect USB device details

lsusb -v | less

Review connected USB devices and verify vendor IDs and product descriptions against expected hardware.

Defensive practice

Use a dedicated security workstation and consider USB whitelisting solutions to prevent unauthorized devices.
