Wi-Fi Pineapple Awareness

Understand how rogue Wi-Fi devices operate and how to detect them defensively.

What it is

A Wi-Fi Pineapple is a device used to create fake access points. Defenders need to recognize the signs of a rogue AP.

Detect suspicious wireless networks

sudo iwlist wlan0 scan | grep -E 'ESSID|Signal'

Look for unexpected SSIDs or multiple access points with similar names in your environment.

Check connected clients

sudo arp -a

Inspect ARP entries for unexpected MAC addresses on your local network.
